Our Menu

Hello! I am excited to introduce you to Kapihan ni Gunyong, a delightful coffee shop nestled in the scenic city of Tagaytay.

Known for our commitment to using high quality coffee beans sourced from local farmers all over the country, we take pride in offering a truly authentic coffee experience that celebrates the flavors and richness of our Philippine coffee culture.

At Kapihan ni Gunyong, we believe that a good cup of coffee is best enjoyed with good food, and we offer a range of delicious dishes to complement your coffee. Whether you are looking for a quick caffeine fix or a relaxing afternoon with friends, our cozy and inviting space is the perfect spot to unwind and indulge in the finer things in life.

So come on over and join us at Kapihan ni Gunyong - we can't wait to share our passion for great coffee and food with you!

History

History of Kapihan ni Gunyong

The success of Kapihan ni Gunyong can be attributed to its commitment to quality and the community. The coffee shop's signature brews are made from beans sourced directly from local farmers, ensuring that the coffee is always fresh and of the highest quality. The owner also goes the extra mile to promote the local coffee industry by educating customers about the history and tradition of coffee farming in the region.

But Kapihan ni Gunyong is more than just a coffee shop. It is a community hub where people come to socialize, relax, and connect with one another. The warm and inviting atmosphere of the shop makes it a popular gathering spot for locals, while its reputation as one of the best coffee shops in the region has made it a must-visit destination for tourists.


Despite its success, Kapihan ni Gunyong remains true to its roots and continues to embody the values of its namesake, Gunyong. The coffee shop remains committed to supporting local farmers and promoting the community's culture and heritage. Its success is a testament to the power of community and the importance of honoring one's roots and traditions.
